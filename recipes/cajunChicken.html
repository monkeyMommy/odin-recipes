Lasagna

A little taste of the south

  • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 Cup medium onion, diced
  • 1 Cup red & Green bell pepper, diced
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 Boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 Cup of shredded cheese
  • 2 Tbsp Cajun seasoning
      Preheat oven at 350*. In a large ovenproof skillet over medium heat, wamt oil. Add onions & peppers until soft, about 5 minutes. Season with salt & pepper. Remove from heat & cool slightly
      With a sharp paring knife, create a pocket in each chicken breast. Stuff with vegetable mixture, then top with cheese. Season all over with Cajun seasoning, salt & pepper.
      Add chicken to skillet & bake until cooked through, about 25 minutes
