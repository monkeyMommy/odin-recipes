Lasagna

This is an image of lasgna it is full of yummy cheese , meat, & tomato sauce. The recipe (not the same source as the picture) is taken off freshsimplehome.com

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Butter
  • Ground Beef
  • No-Boil Lasagna
      Preheat oven at 350*. While waiting brown the beef & drain the grease.
      Lay out all the ingredients to layer. First spread 1/4 cup of pasta sauce along the base of dish.
      Spread 1/3 of the browned beef evenly
      Now, layer 1/4 of the cheese
      Place the noodles sheets on top, breaking or cutting if necessary to fit pan
      Place dabs of butter. Repeat all the above steps twice more
      Sprinkle the last of the cheese on top
      Bake for 40 minutes. Enjoy!
